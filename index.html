◎ Circle Card Game

Circle Card Game

6 players sit around a circular board divided into two halves — White team (top: WL, WC, WR) and Black team (bottom: BL, BC, BR). Each player owns one sector. A token starts at center, randomly colored white or black each round.

Turn Order

WC↔BC · WL↔BL · WR↔BR. The first player in each pair plays, then their opposite responds. Pairs cycle until all cards are gone or the token exits the board.

Cards

Each player draws cards per round from a shared deck. Movement is always radial from your perspective — OUT pushes away, IN pulls toward you.

⬆⬆ OUT2 — push 2 rings away
⬇⬇ IN2 — pull 2 rings toward
⬆↻ OUT1CW — out 1 + rotate CW
⬆↺ OUT1CCW — out 1 + rotate CCW
⬇↻ IN1CW — in 1 + rotate CW
⬇↺ IN1CCW — in 1 + rotate CCW
↺↻ SIDE2 — slide 2 sectors CW or CCW
✦ CHOICE — move 1 step in any direction
⇄ COLORSWAP — flip token color
✦✦ DRAW2 — draw 2 cards, skip move
⚑ TRAP — place on a sector; triggers hit effect when token lands there (once)
Hitting & HP

Token crossing the outer ring hits that sector's player. Token color matches team → heal 1 HP. Doesn't match → 1 damage. Traps trigger the same effect mid-board without ending the round.

Optional Rules
◆ 1v5 FFA — all 6 compete individually
◆ Sector turn order — next to play is whoever owns the token's current sector
◆ Center flip — token landing at center flips its color
◆ Configurable HP & hand size — 1–5 each
